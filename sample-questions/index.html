雅思考试样题

上海高中生入门版 · IELTS Academic 原创模拟题

说明：以下题目用于快速了解雅思听、读、写、说四科常见形式，不是官方真题。

1. Listening 听力

Section 1：报名信息填写

Receptionist: Good morning, Greenfield Sports Centre.
Student: I’d like to register for the summer swimming course.
Student: I’m sixteen.
Receptionist: The course begins on the 18th of July. Classes are held every Tuesday and Thursday at 6:30 p.m.
Receptionist: The full fee is 480 yuan.
  1. Age: ______
  2. Start date: ______ July
  3. Class days: Tuesday and ______
  4. Start time: ______ p.m.
  5. Course fee: ______ yuan
Answers: 16 · 18th · Thursday · 6:30 · 480

2. Reading 阅读

The Rise of Urban Cycling

In many large cities, cycling has become increasingly popular as a form of daily transport. Bicycles are relatively inexpensive compared with cars. They also require less space and produce no direct air pollution.

Research shows that people are more likely to cycle when routes are connected, safe and convenient. A single bicycle lane that suddenly ends at a busy road may do little to increase cycling.

Bicycle-sharing systems allow users to rent a bicycle for a short journey and return it elsewhere. These systems can reduce traffic congestion and improve public health, although maintenance costs can be high.

True / False / Not Given

  1. Bicycles cost more than cars.
  2. Safe and connected routes can encourage cycling.
  3. All bicycle lanes increase cycling.
  4. Bicycle-sharing systems are mainly used by tourists.
Answers: False · True · False · Not Given

Overall, the proportion of students using public transport increased in all three cities. Shanghai experienced the largest rise and became the city with the highest percentage in 2025, while Sydney remained the lowest.

4. Speaking 口语

Part 1

  • What subjects do you enjoy studying?
  • What would you like to study at university?
  • Has your hometown changed recently?
  • Is technology sometimes distracting?

Part 2

Describe a teacher who has influenced you.

  • who the teacher is
  • what subject the teacher teaches
  • what the teacher did
  • why the teacher influenced you

Part 3

  • What makes a good teacher?
  • Should students use AI tools for homework?
